Sunday Pot Roast

Delicious slow roasted beef and veggies

Fall apart, tender Pot Roast slow cooked and smothered in a delicious gravy with potatoes and carrots. A warm and hearty dinner recipeâ€¦ this beef roast is a perfect complete meal you can prepare with minimum effort.

Ingredients

  • One 3-5 pound chuck roast
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 whole carrots peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 2 sprigs rosemary and thyme

Steps

  1. Heat skillet until high heat
  2. Sear the roast on the skillet
  3. Once all sides are seared place roast into a crockpot slowcooker
  4. Add all other ingredients to the slowcooker
  5. Set the slowcooker to 10 hours and simmer all day
